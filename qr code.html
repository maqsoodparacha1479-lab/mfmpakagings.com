    Frequently Asked Questions about Customize your QR code

    Frames add a visual border and a call-to-action like “Scan Me”, helping your QR code attract attention and guiding users to interact with it. Using frames can significantly increase the scan rate, especially in marketing campaigns and printed materials.

    Our designs are optimized for perfect readability. As long as key QR elements remain clear, your code will scan instantly. ME-QR automatically checks scannability, so you can safely use creative designs without risking usability.

    Unique patterns and shapes give your QR code a branded, premium look and help it stand out against standard black-and-white squares. This visual uniqueness catches the eye and increases the likelihood that users will scan your code.

    Use high-contrast colors, where the QR pattern is darker than the background. Branded color schemes help strengthen your visual identity, but always ensure readability to maintain fast scanning across all devices.

    You can download your customized QR code in PNG, JPG, SVG, or PDF depending on where you plan to use it. This ensures high quality for both digital use and professional printing.
